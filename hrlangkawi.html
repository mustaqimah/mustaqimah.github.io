Langkawi

When we have a vacation to Langkawi Island, finding budget accommodation and affordable hotels in Langkawi especially around the beach is really easy but not all good. Everyone including myself want to stay at comfortable place and is worth the price even cheap. And usually when traveling here we should look for a affordable hotel near the Langkawi Airport.

Besides comfort and price, another factor that we must consider is to find a cheap hotel location. You may not want to stay in a cheap and comfortable hotel but also to the nearby attractions. Here is a list of affordable hotels in Langkawi which have good facilities and good reviews and are in the vicinity of tourist attractions in Langkawi such as the beach area of Cenang, Kuah Town and Langkawi Airport.
