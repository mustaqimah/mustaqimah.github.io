Day 1:Arrival in Kota Kinabalu – Kota Kinabalu City Tour – Jambatan Tamparuli – Rumah Terbalik

Arrive in the early morning at the Kota Kinabalu Airport (BKI). Proceed for pickup from the airport terminal and head to the alluring town of Kota Kinabalu for breakfast. After breakfast, proceed for a half day city tour to various landmarks of Kota Kinabalu.

The tour starts with the Floating Mosque, one of the few unique floating Islamic houses of worship in Malaysia. The structure in itself is a sight to behold, as the perfect symphony of sea and light reflects its pearl white shadow onto the surrounding man-made lagoon. Then, head over to Tun Mustapha Tower or the Sabah Foundation Building, is a 30 storey circular glass marvel of architecture. Constructed in 1977, it towers 122 meters across the Sabah skyline, and is one of the few hanging type structures in the world. Visitors can marvel at the modern architecture and take beautiful photos of the scenic view.

Floating Mosque
Tun Mustapha Tower

After lunch, proceed towards the Poh Toh Tze Temple. The magnificent Buddhist temple is popular to many of the local Chinese worshipers, with its vividly carved rooftops imbued with elegant Eastern culture, as well as the pillars and walls that have a distinct Chinese flavor. Inside the compounds lay 10 large statues of dainties including the Goodness of Mercy who welcomes all who enters the temple. After that, head back to the hotel for check-in and for some quality relaxing time. Refresh and unwind from the busy morning.

Poh Toh Tze Temple

After that, proceed onwards to Tamparuli town to the unique architecture known as ‘Rumah Terbalik’ or the Upside Down House. The first of its kind in South East Asia, it is among the seven such reversed structures in the world, and commands a great deal of interest due to its distinct construction methods. Don’t forget to stop by at jambatan Tamparuli, the most famous bridge of Sabah Tamparuli hanging bridge. In the evening, proceed to the Asia City Complex for a taste of the local seafood scene. Dig into fresh catches of fresh prawns, red snappers, squid and other amazing delicacies that will sure tingle your taste buds. Head back to the hotel for a good night’s rest and the seafood dinner.

Rumah Terbalik
Jambatan Tamparuli

Day 2:Kinabalu National Park – Kundasang War Memorial – Poring Hot Spring – Tagal Fish Spa – Desa Cattle Farm

After breakfast at the resort, proceed to Kinabalu National Park for a presentation at Exhibition Hall. Enjoy the magnificent allure of Mount Kinabalu, the tallest peak in South East Asia, its towering peak high above the clouds.The park is home to many endemic species of flora and fauna, including Nepenthes Rajah, the largest pitcher plant in the world, fascinating orchids and Borneo’s montane rainforest birds. Stop over at Kundasang War Memorial, the first memorials to commemorate the brave Australian and British Prisoners of War who died in Sandakan and during the infamous death marches to Ranau during World War II.

Kinabalu National Park
Kundasang War Memorial

At the town of Tamparuli, visit the local food stalls for brunch. Here, have a taste of the renowned Mee Tuaran. Proceed to Poring Hot Springs situated at the foothills of Mount Kinabalu. Moving on, immerse in the warm waters of Poring Hot Springs which are rich in sulphuric minerals. The natural spring water, which has a very positive and therapeutic effect stimulates the body and helps ease muscle aches. A must visit is the Treetop Canopy Walkway just 20 minutes hike away. Experience walking 40 metres above the rainforest floor.

Poring Hot Spring

Thereafter, proceed to Kampung Luanti for a fish massage or fish spa at the Tagal Fish Spa. The therapeutic method of fish feeding uses the special breed of Malaysian Mahseer that is trained by the village locals to feed on dead skin and rough pores. After a recuperative session, proceed to the Desa Cattle Farm. One can see the processes of dairy farms – including feeding, milking of cows at the ranch, as well as visit the calf pen to see the younglings. There are also fresh milk cartons on sale, together with throngs of trinkets, souvenirs and gifts at the gift store for memories. After a full day of fruitful tour, proceed to a nearby restaurant for a sumptuous dinner. Take an early rest in the hotel after dinner.

Tagal Fish Spa
Desa Cattle Farm

Day 3:Sapi Island and Manukan Island – Tanjung Aru Beach

Wake up to a wholesome breakfast at the hotel, and freshen up for the next adventure. Next, head on to the Jesselton Point Jetty. Home to an array of harbored vassals and ferries, the landmark is a fantastic sight to behold, showcasing an incorporation of colonial as well as modern architectural design. Hiring a boat for island hopping and tour the remote and tranquil islands around the continent. Take the next available boat out to the Tunku Abdul Rahman Park. You should visit some of the most beautiful cluster of islands Sapi Island and Manukan Island. A chartered speed boat is arranged to take visitors to the islands, which boast pristine shallow waters, rich coral gardens and fine, white sandy beaches.

Sapi Island and Manukan Island, Kota Kinabalu

At noon, enjoy a sumptuous BBQ lunch on the islands at the designated dinner hall. Unwind and rest the tired soul.

After lunch, continues with more options to snorkel by the shorelines, or simply just watch the waves brush against the shoreline in complete serenity. One can also have a go for scuba diving, as well as opt to partake in the various sea sports activities provided on the island (includes banana boat, parasailing, jet skiing and more). After a relaxing island tour for the day, pack up your belongings and gather at the jetty. The boat will bring the group safely back to Jesselton Point Jetty, and proceed for a transfer to the hotel. Refresh and relax, as the rest of the evening is at free-and-easy.

In the evening, head on to the Tanjung Aru Beach. Stretching at over 2 kilometers long the serene beachfront of white sand and warm tropical sun makes it a wonderful setting for relaxation. Enjoy the sunset view. Then, go back to hotel and take some rest.

Tanjung Aru Beach

Day 4:Pekan Nabalu – Signal Hill – Atkinson Clock Tower – Filipino Market – Departure

At the last day, head back to kota kinabalu for city sightseeing and shopping. After breakfast at the resort, free at leisure. During transfer to Kota Kinabalu, a brief stop at Pekan Nabalu where a rich variety of local fruits, vegetables and handicraft items are being sold. Brief city tour to Signal Hill Observatory for a fantastic scenic view of Kota Kinabalu city and its surrounding islands. A hilltop point which gives one a birds’ eye view of the panoramic Kota Kinabalu city skyline, its vast surrounding greenery and exotic tropical islands. Drive through Atkinson Clock Tower and brief stop at Filipino Market. Proceed to airport.

Atkinson Clock Tower
