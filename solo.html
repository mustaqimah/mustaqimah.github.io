Solo traveller: safest place to go out of country

Why travel solo, there are many reasons that can be relate. Its either your travels partner could not fit with your schedule, you need freedom and new experience where you can get out from your comfort zone while trying new things.

1. Reykjavik, Iceland


It is one of the safest place to travel alone mainly for woman and had been ranked as first place on Global Peace Index. Reykjavik is a great base for day trips taht display Iceland's perfect attraction such as the Blue Lagoon and Northern Lights.

2. Montreal, Canada


Its scenic riverfront Old Port, history museums, grand old French restaurants and old cobblestone streets that are lined beautifully with 17th-century buildings had always captured the travelers heart without need to spend much. The Canadians are friendly and charming, making Montreal one of the safest and best places to travel alone in North American. In addition, it only just a few hours away from the splendid Niagara Falls.

3. Singapore


It friendly, safe and super clean environment will mesmerized everyone in the blink of an eye. For those who will spend their days for below than 3 days, you can always ask for Singapore Tourist Pass by the MRT Ticket Office. It will definitely save your pocket while you can travel by using their public transport limitless times. Their Garden by The Bay, Singapore Flyer, Orcard Road, Merlion Park will always make you fall in love with this country more and more. For adventure lovers, you can go straight to Sentosa Island where Universal Studio and Adventure Park take place.
